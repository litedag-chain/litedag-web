The emission schedule of the LiteDAG Protocol balances three core principles:

- **Fairness**: Equitable distribution, preventing excessive accumulation by early participants.
- **Security**: Robust block rewards that persist long enough to secure the network.
- **Sustainability**: Rapidly reducing inflation to a minimal, sustainable level.

LiteDAG launched with **~196.4 million LDG** from the Virel snapshot (1:1 balance migration). New supply is created through block rewards, with a perpetual tail emission ensuring miners are always incentivized.

#### A Smooth, Predictable Decline

Unlike Bitcoin's abrupt "halving" events, LiteDAG's block reward decreases smoothly by **10% every season** (91 days). This provides miners with a predictable and gradual reduction in income, encouraging consistent mining activity.

Key features of the emission schedule:

- **Initial Block Reward**: **20 LDG** per block (15-second block time, ~115,200 LDG mined daily).
- **Treasury Fee**: 10% of each block reward funds ongoing protocol development.
- **Initial Stability Period**: The block reward remains at **20 LDG for the first six months** (two seasons) before reductions begin.
- **Regular Reductions**: Starting from the sixth month, the block reward decreases by 10% every season.
- **Tail Emission**: The block reward never drops below **1 LDG per block** (~2.1 million LDG/year), ensuring perpetual miner incentive and long-term network security.

#### Supply Overview

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Virel snapshot (prefunded at genesis) | ~196.4M LDG |
| New emission from block rewards | ~115.3M LDG (before tail) |
| Tail emission | 1 LDG/block perpetual (~2.1M/year) |

#### Reward Distribution

Each block reward is split:

| Recipient | Share | At 20 LDG/block |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| PoW Miner | 50% | 10 LDG |
| PoS Staker | 40% | 8 LDG |
| Treasury | 10% | 2 LDG |

If no staker is active, the staker share is burned — incentivizing staking participation.
